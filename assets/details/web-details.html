"Development of Faculty Profiling with Data Analytics for City College of Tagaytay"

Faculty Profiling system is a system that manage the records of every faculty member in the institution. This includes the personal information and other data that the faculty members have. Data analytics is the process of examining data sets in order to find trends and draw conclusions about the information they contain in order to help analyzing the data and to make a better decision. Data Analytics is usually used by scientist and researchers to support theories and hypothesis, it is important in research because it makes data analysis much easier and more precise it enables researchers to clearly evaluate data ensuring that nothing is missed out that could prevent them from drawing conclusions from it. Data Analysis is the process of systematically applying statistical and/or logical techniques to describe and illustrate, condense and recap, and evaluate data.
